OVERVIEW

Set in an area with restaurants, cafes and duty-free shopping, this relaxed hotel is 4 km from Olongapo City bus station and 7 km from 
Baloy Beach. The unpretentious rooms and suites provide complimentary Internet access, flat-screen TVs and minifridges, as well as tea 
and coffee making facilities. Breakfast is free. Other amenities consist of an outdoor pool and a fitness room. Event space includes a ballroom.
