Some of the Reviews

Maynard R.
Oxnard, United States

They specialize in traditional Filipino fare. The food is cooked to order, so you have to be patient. But it is worth the wait. The food was served smoking hot, and packed full of flavor. They have a pretty good selection of beverages as well, but I highly recommend the iced tea, it's fricking good!, specially in hot and humid conditions that Subic is in. The place is air conditioned, so it's nice and cool while you're inside. It's a relief from the nearly unbearable heat and humidity. This place is located just steps away from they Harbor Point Mall, so expect the surrounding area to be crowded, specially during the weekends. I don't know how long it'll be before I can visit the Philippines again. But when I do go the next time, and if this place will still be there, I'm definitely coming back!
